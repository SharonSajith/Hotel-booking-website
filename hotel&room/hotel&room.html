Hilton Hotel

Linked by a walkway to Colombo's World Trade Center, this modern hotel is a 9-minute walk from Fort railway station and a 12-minute walk from the beach. Bright, warm rooms offer Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Some have ocean views. Suites and club rooms gain access to a lounge offering breakfast and snacks; they also feature separate living rooms. Room service is available. Dining options include a colonial-style pub, a karaoke bar and an Italian restaurant. There's also a gym and a beauty salon, as well as an outdoor pool with a kids' pool and a bar. Parking is available

Kandalama Hotel

Set among forest alongside the Kandalama Reservoir, this upscale resort is 4 km from the A6 road, and 9 km from Dambulla Royal Cave Temple and Golden Temple. Polished villas have Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and forest views, as well as minifridges, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Elegant suites offer sitting areas, and balconies or terraces; upgraded suites feature private plunge pools. Butler service and room service is 24/7. Amenities include a restaurant, and a bar with regular live music, plus an Ayurvedic spa and an outdoor pool. Breakfast is available.

Galle Beach Hotel

A few steps from Pitiwella Beach in the center of Galle Smile Beach Home provides accommodations with access to beauty services. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant hairdresser's and spa facilities. The vacation home offers sea views a sun terrace a 24-hour front desk and free Wifi is available throughout the property. Each room comes with a safety deposit box while certain rooms will provide you with a balcony. At the vacation home each unit comes with a seating area. There is a snack bar and packed lunches are also available. Yoga classes and fitness classes are provided at the fitness room on site.
